When an upgrade matters

Upgrade only when the current plan starts limiting real workflow needs.

Stay on the current plan when

You are still validating the workflow, running a limited number of sessions, and do not need decision-package export yet.

Move to Pro when

Your team needs larger monthly throughput, exportable decision packages, or more room to operate without plan-based interruptions.
